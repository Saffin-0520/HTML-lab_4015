Revolutionize Your Ride

The Yamaha FZ-X is the perfect combination of style, comfort, and performance. Explore the urban streets or the open highways with this powerful and stylish bike.

Key Features

  • Neo-Retro Styling: A bold and retro design fused with modern technology.
  • 149cc Fuel-Injected Engine: Smooth performance for city rides and weekend adventures.
  • LED Lighting: Bright and clear vision with advanced LED headlight and DRL.
  • Comfortable Ride: Well-cushioned seat and upright riding posture for long rides.

Specifications

Engine Type Air-cooled, 4-stroke, SOHC, 2-valve
Displacement 149cc
Maximum Power 12.4 PS @ 7250 rpm
Maximum Torque 13.3 Nm @ 5500 rpm
Fuel Tank Capacity 10 liters
